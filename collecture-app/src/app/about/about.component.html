University-Grade Education For All.

  • In an unprecedented move, universities across the globe are hosting thousands of virtual lectures each week.
  • Wouldn't it be great if we could preserve and share this knowledge?
  • These lectures are already recorded in digital formats, so it's just a few steps to make them accessible to the world.
  • Collecture aims to collect these lectures in one place, tagging and organizing them so anyone can search and find exactly what they need.
